God of War Ragnarök

Overview

God of War Ragnarök is the epic continuation of Kratos and Atreus’s journey through the Norse realms. Developed by Santa Monica Studio, the game delivers an emotionally charged narrative, breathtaking visuals, and brutal combat as the father-son duo face the impending doom of Ragnarök and battle gods like Thor and Odin.

Details

  • Developer: Santa Monica Studio
  • Release Date: November 9, 2022
  • Platforms: PlayStation 5, PlayStation 4
  • Genres: Action-Adventure, Hack and Slash
