Assassin's Creed Shadows

Overview

Set in feudal Japan, Assassin's Creed Shadows introduces players to a new open-world adventure featuring dual protagonists — a stealthy shinobi and a fierce samurai. Developed by Ubisoft, this installment promises deep storytelling, breathtaking landscapes, and refined parkour and combat mechanics.

Details

  • Developer: Ubisoft
  • Release Date: November 2025 (TBA)
  • Platforms: PlayStation 5, Xbox Series X|S, PC
  • Genres: Action-Adventure, Stealth
