Ghost of Yotei

Overview

Ghost of Yotei is a spiritual successor to samurai-inspired action games, set in a mythical version of Japan. Players take on the role of a fallen warrior reborn as a vengeful spirit, exploring snow-covered mountains, haunted villages, and ancient temples. With a mix of stealth, swordplay, and supernatural abilities, Ghost of Yotei promises an intense and atmospheric experience.

Details

  • Developer: Moonshade Studios
  • Release Date: TBA
  • Platforms: PlayStation 5, Xbox Series X|S, PC
  • Genres: Action-Adventure, Stealth, Fantasy
