Hollow Knight: Silksong

Overview

Hollow Knight: Silksong is the highly anticipated sequel to the critically acclaimed Hollow Knight. Developed by Team Cherry, the game follows Hornet, the princess-protector of Hallownest, as she journeys through a new kingdom haunted by silk and song. Explore stunning hand-drawn environments, battle terrifying enemies, and uncover deep lore in this action-packed metroidvania experience.

Details

  • Developer: Team Cherry
  • Release Date: TBA
  • Platforms: Nintendo Switch, PC, Xbox, PlayStation
  • Genres: Metroidvania, Action, Indie
