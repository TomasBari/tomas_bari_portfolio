Horizon Forbidden West

Overview

Horizon Forbidden West is the sequel to the hit action RPG Horizon Zero Dawn. Developed by Guerrilla Games, the game continues the story of Aloy, a young huntress in a world overrun by mysterious robotic creatures. Explore the majestic landscapes of the western frontier, discover new tribes, and face powerful new threats in this expansive open-world adventure.

Details

  • Developer: Guerrilla Games
  • Release Date: February 18, 2022
  • Platforms: PlayStation 5, PlayStation 4
  • Genres: Action RPG, Open World
