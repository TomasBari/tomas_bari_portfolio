Baldur's Gate 3

Overview

Baldur's Gate 3 is an epic role-playing game developed by Larian Studios, based on the Dungeons & Dragons universe. It offers deep narrative choices, tactical turn-based combat, and a vast world to explore. Players will experience rich storytelling with complex characters and dynamic multiplayer options.

Details

  • Developer: Larian Studios
  • Release Date: August 2023
  • Platforms: PC, PlayStation 5
  • Genres: RPG, Turn-based strategy
