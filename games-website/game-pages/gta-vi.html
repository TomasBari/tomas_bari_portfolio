Grand Theft Auto VI

Overview

Grand Theft Auto VI marks the highly anticipated return of Rockstar Games' open-world crime saga. Set in a modern-day Vice City-inspired environment, players follow the story of two protagonists as they navigate the criminal underworld. With stunning visuals, dynamic world interactions, and a revamped AI system, GTA VI aims to push the limits of what an open-world game can be.

Details

  • Developer: Rockstar Games
  • Release Date: 2025 (TBA)
  • Platforms: PlayStation 5, Xbox Series X|S
  • Genres: Action-Adventure, Open World
