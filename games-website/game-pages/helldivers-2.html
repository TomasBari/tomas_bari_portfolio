Helldivers 2

Overview

Helldivers 2 is the explosive co-op third-person shooter from Arrowhead Game Studios. Set in a satirical, militaristic sci-fi universe, players fight for “managed democracy” across hostile alien worlds. Featuring brutal difficulty, strategic teamwork, and chaotic combat, Helldivers 2 expands on its predecessor with enhanced graphics, dynamic missions, and real-time galactic war progress.

Details

  • Developer: Arrowhead Game Studios
  • Publisher: Sony Interactive Entertainment
  • Release Date: February 8, 2024
  • Platforms: PlayStation 5, PC
  • Genres: Co-op Shooter, Action, Sci-Fi
