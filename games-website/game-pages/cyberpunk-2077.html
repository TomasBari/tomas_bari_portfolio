Cyberpunk 2077

Overview

Cyberpunk 2077, developed by CD Projekt Red, is an open-world RPG set in the dystopian Night City. Players navigate a rich narrative filled with high-tech weaponry, cybernetic enhancements, and complex characters.

Details

  • Developer: CD Projekt Red
  • Release Date: December 10, 2020
  • Platforms: PC, PlayStation 4, PlayStation 5, Xbox One, Xbox Series X|S
  • Genres: Action RPG, Open World
