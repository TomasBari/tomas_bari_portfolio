Elden Ring

Overview

Elden Ring is a groundbreaking action RPG from FromSoftware and George R. R. Martin. Set in the Lands Between, the game offers vast exploration, epic bosses, and deep lore, all wrapped in the studio’s signature challenging gameplay and dark fantasy storytelling.

Details

  • Developer: FromSoftware
  • Release Date: February 25, 2022
  • Platforms: PC, PlayStation 4/5, Xbox One, Xbox Series X|S
  • Genres: Action RPG, Open World, Fantasy
