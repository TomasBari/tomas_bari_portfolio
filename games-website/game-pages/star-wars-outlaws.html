Star Wars Outlaws

Overview

Star Wars Outlaws is the first open-world Star Wars game, taking place between Empire Strikes Back and Return of the Jedi. Players take on the role of Kay Vess, a clever scoundrel navigating the galaxy’s criminal underworld. The game offers space and land-based exploration, choices that influence the story, and dynamic stealth-action gameplay.

Details

  • Developer: Massive Entertainment (Ubisoft)
  • Release Date: August 30, 2024
  • Platforms: PlayStation 5, Xbox Series X|S, PC
  • Genres: Action-Adventure, Open World
