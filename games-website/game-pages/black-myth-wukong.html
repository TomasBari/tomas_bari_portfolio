Black Myth: Wukong

Overview

Black Myth: Wukong is an action RPG developed by Game Science, inspired by the classic Chinese novel "Journey to the West." The game features intense combat, a richly detailed world, and stunning visuals powered by Unreal Engine 5. Players take on the role of the legendary Monkey King, exploring mythological realms and battling fearsome creatures.

Details

  • Developer: Game Science
  • Release Date: 2024 (TBA)
  • Platforms: PC, PlayStation 5
  • Genres: Action RPG
